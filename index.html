    How are we protecting them?

    The conservation of red pandas relies heavily on local communities.
    We work with individuals who live among red pandas to ensure that
    their livelihoods are stable and that they coexist peacefully with the
    surrounding wildlife.

    Why save the red panda?

    Unique

    Red pandas are the only living member of their own taxonomic family.

    Important

    Red pandas are a flagship species of a biodiversity hotspot.

    Endangered

    Our planet has lost half of its wild red pandas in 20 years.



    Our Mission

    Red Panda Sanctuary is committed to the conservation of wild red pandas and their habitat through education and empowerment of local communities.
